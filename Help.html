Quick Answers
Frequently Asked

Common questions about your shipments, payments, and account.

How do I track my vehicle shipment?
You can track your vehicle's progress directly from the Dashboard. Each vehicle shows its current stage from purchase through to delivery. Click on any vehicle row to see the full stage timeline.
How do I add a payment to my wallet?
Navigate to the Wallet tab and click "Add Payment". Upload your bank receipt, enter the transaction ID, select currency and amount, then submit. Our team will verify and confirm within 24 hours.
What is a Bill of Lading and how does it work?
A Bill of Lading (BL) is a legal shipping document. You can choose either Surrender (buyer collects from origin) or Dispatch (courier to buyer's address). Select your preferred method in Stage 6 of the vehicle timeline.
How do I request a consignee change?
From the Dashboard, click the "C" badge in the consignee column for any vehicle. Then click "Request Consignee Change". Fill in the new details and submit — our sales team will review and approve before the BL is amended.
Why is my payment showing as Rejected?
Payments may be rejected if the bank receipt is unclear, the amount doesn't match, or the transaction ID is incorrect. Check the rejection reason in your Wallet, correct the issue, and submit a new payment request.
How long does shipping typically take?
Shipping timelines vary by destination. Typically Japan to Middle East takes 3–4 weeks, to Africa 5–7 weeks. Your vehicle's stage timeline on the Dashboard will show estimated milestones for your specific shipment.
